\section{Standard Model}

Every known matter constitutes of point-like particles without further internal structure, called elementary particles. These mainly interact with each other trough three elementary forces: The strong, the weak and the electromagnetic interaction. The standard model summarises the quantum field theories describing these interactions and classifies the elementary particles by their quantum numbers. \cite{standard_model}
